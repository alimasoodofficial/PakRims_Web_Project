2024 Toyota Yaris Facelift In Pakistan – Test Drive

By Ali Masood On Jun 10, 2024

Level-Up Exterior

Parent company Toyota has claimed that the overall front fascia of the Yaris facelift is inspired by the hammerhead shark ‘wide and impactful’. Other exterior updates are new fog lamps for improved visibility, modern LED headlamps and DRLs with a ‘follow me home’ feature, new radiator grille, sport alloy rims, redesigned front and rear bumpers, shark fin antenna for improved aerodynamics, smart door handles, rear parking sensors, and retractable mirrors.

Premium Interior

The modified interior gives you two color options: beige and black. Where we chose the black top line variant for its extra-ordinary exquisite appeal. Facelift Yaris comes with polyurethane foam to provide better driving comfort. The premium feel is enhanced with a leather-stitched steering wheel. The old Yaris 7-inch infotainment screen has been replaced by the new 9-inch floating screen, with the addition of Android Auto and Apple CarPlay. Luxurious appeal is added by bringing cushioning on doors. Moreover, the top-line variants now have push start option too. Also, the cruise control is added to assist the driver in speeding up to the custom-limit pre-set.
